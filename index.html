About me

Hi! My name is Julie. I am a Toronto based Front-End Developer. I love to solve problems using clean, semantic code and solid logic. I am passionate about creating interactive web experiences that inspire and engage users.

I have a Bachelor's degree in Science and in the past I have worked in the pharmaceutical industry in different capacities, mainly in quality control and sterilization validation/sterility assurance. I also love anything art related and I like to be creative. I felt I could merge my interest in science and technology with art and creativity into one focus: web development.
